How does it work?

TechMine takes a different approach to how you share and access your files. Instead of storing your files on our servers, which would potentially expose your files to unwanted viewers, we setup a direct connection between the devices. A device downloads or streams a file directly from the device that owns that file. This approach gives you more control and is quicker.
